Who We Are

Global Message Services (GMS) is a communications platform provider (CPaaS) for Enterprises and an international messaging service provider for Mobile Network Operators worldwide.

We bring the mobile messaging ecosystem together by providing a single access point for messaging traffic exchange between enterprises and mobile operators.

For Enterprises, our communications platform (CPaaS) enables brands to unify customer communications via a single API and self-service platform, making it simple to send and receive messages orchestrated across multichannel touchpoints – including SMS, Push, Viber, WhatsApp, and RCS.

As a trusted partner of mobile operators, we enable their messaging business by optimising A2P, P2P and P2A interworking while enhancing enterprise A2P messaging traffic management for revenue growth.

GMS is headquartered in Baar, Switzerland, with regional offices worldwide.

GMS was founded in 2006 as a P2P messaging hub for the CIS region.

Soon, the company realised that its sprawling network of direct connections with mobile operators in the CIS was a perfect stepping stone for transforming GMS into the region’s first and most successful A2P messaging hub. GMS’ growing expertise led it to pioneer domestic and international traffic segregation in this immense market.

After eight years of ample growth in the CIS, GMS was ready for the next big step. In 2014, it refocused its attention towards the global market, relocating its HQ to Switzerland and giving a new boost to its expansion.

Paving the road to success, GMS set up many new direct connections with MNOs and collaborations with some of the world’s largest tech enterprises. Expanding its CPaaS offering, GMS started an official partnership with Rakuten Viber in 2015, and continues to help enterprises build meaningful interactions with their clients, further developing its communication solutions.

As the preferred global partner of over 900 mobile operators and over 400 enterprises worldwide, GMS continues to grow its client base, pursue product innovations, and expand its extensive network of connections while ensuring safe and transparent traffic routing.

Iurii Makarenko

President of the Board / Executive Chairman of the Board

Iurii has been working in the telecoms world for more than 25 years. After earning a management and marketing degree from the Igor Sikorsky Kyiv Polytechnic Institute, he worked at Utel and MTS Ukraine from 1995 to 2006. Here he was in charge of expanding the fixed-line and mobile carriers’ worldwide business, including messaging. In this time, Iurii strengthened his comprehensive understanding of the industry, as well as the opportunities it presented.

Knowing the challenges MNOs face, he saw the market’s need for a customer-focused messaging company to revolutionise the industry. Iurii sought to fill this gap, and in 2006 together with a team of fellow experts, founded Global Message Services — a new and innovative company to help shape the future of the messaging ecosystem and beyond.

GMS has since grown from a local company operating in the CIS market to a global leader in the messaging services sector in its 15 years under Iurii’s guidance. In early 2020, he assumed the position of President and Executive Chairman of the Board and is now focused on GMS’ long-term strategic development.

Iurii loves to exercise and the great outdoors and spends his free time playing tennis, rowing, running, cycling and walking his dog.

Illia Solodovskyi

Member of the Board / Chief Growth Officer

Illia is one of the founding members of GMS and plays a pivotal role in our continued success. He now serves as our Chief Growth Officer and a member of the board.

In 1992 Illia graduated with a degree in Radio Engineering from Odessa Polytechnic University and has been working in the telecoms industry ever since. Over the years, he held many positions from Chief Engineer to Managing Director of several telecommunications companies and founded several businesses including Priocom – a Ukrainian systems integration company for telecom operators and large enterprises, and Atracom – the biggest developer and fibre optic network provider in Ukraine with more than 23 000 kilometres of FOCL. Eventually, he brought that wealth of knowledge and experience to help co-found GMS in 2006.

Illia’s business interests are not limited to just telecommunications. In 2014, he co-founded a professional cloud-based platform called UNIP, that provides cities and drivers with a comprehensive parking and mobility management solution.

Outside of work, Illia was also a member of the Ukrainian national athletics team and still loves to run. He also plays tennis and is a musician, playing the piano, trumpet, and guitar. He takes pride in sponsoring young and talented tennis players and musicians.
